🌶️ Basic Indian-Style Curry Recipe 🧂 Ingredients: Oil – 2 tbsp (vegetable or ghee) Onion – 1 large, finely chopped Garlic – 3 cloves, minced Ginger – 1 inch, grated Tomato – 2 medium, chopped or pureed Green chili – 1 (optional, adjust to taste) 🌿 Spices: Turmeric powder – ½ tsp Red chili powder – 1 tsp Coriander powder – 1 tsp Cumin seeds – ½ tsp Garam masala – ½ tsp Salt – to taste 🍛 Main Ingredients (Choose one or mix): Vegetables (potato, peas, cauliflower, carrots, etc.) Chicken (boneless or bone-in) Paneer or tofu Chickpeas or lentils (cooked) 🥣 Liquid: Water – 1 cup (adjust for thickness) Or coconut milk – 1 cup (for a creamy curry) 🌿 Optional Garnish: Fresh coriander (cilantro), chopped A squeeze of lemon 👨‍🍳 Instructions: Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them sizzle. Add chopped onion and sauté until golden brown. Add ginger, garlic, and green chili. Cook until the raw smell goes away. Add chopped or pureed tomatoes and cook until oil separates. Add all spices: turmeric, red chili, coriander powder, and salt. Stir well. Add your main ingredients (vegetables, chicken, chickpeas, etc.). Mix to coat with the masala. Pour in water or coconut milk. Cover and cook: Vegetables: 10–15 min or until soft Chicken: 20–25 min until fully cooked Chickpeas: 5–10 min if already cooked Finish with garam masala and let simmer 2–3 more minutes. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot. 🍚 Serve With: Steamed rice Chapati, naan, or paratha
